I was part of a team of other Computer Science students in a locally held hackathon, Valley Hacks, with the theme of ducation and finance. What I gained: Experience collaborating with a team of unfamiliar programmers, knowledge about developing in Python, understanding of blockchain and it's high-level implementation.
